FAQ

Answers, before you ask.

Is my family's data really private?

Yes. We use end‑to‑end encryption for all messages, never sell data, and never train models on your family's conversations. You can export or delete everything at any time.

What ages is this appropriate for?

The agent adapts its tone for ages 8 and up. For kids under 8, parents see a transcript by default and the agent uses simpler language. Teen mode unlocks more privacy at age 13 — with parental controls intact.

Do my kids need a smartphone?

No. Riley works over plain SMS, iMessage, WhatsApp, and our optional app. If they can text, they can use it — including kids on smartwatches.

How does it handle co‑parents?

You can add up to four parent‑level accounts with custom permission rules. The agent knows whose week it is and routes requests accordingly — never picking sides.

What if the AI gets something wrong?

Every action is logged and reversible for 30 days. For anything irreversible (signing forms, making purchases), the agent confirms with a parent first.

What schools and apps do you integrate with?

Canvas, PowerSchool, Schoology, Google Classroom, ClassDojo, Apple Family, Google Family Link, iCloud Calendar, Google Calendar, Outlook, Life360, and 30+ more. We add new integrations every month — just ask.
